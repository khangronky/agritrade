Before:
Farmer → Middleman → Buyer

After:
Farmer → (Platform) → Buyer
    ↘ Middleman (optional)


Target 1: Improve Market Transparency
Pain: Farmers lack visibility on prices and buyers
Tactics:
Provide real-time price updates by region


Show multiple buyer price offers


Display simple price trends (up/down)


Target 2: Reduce Search Effort for Farmers
Pain: Farmers spend time and effort finding buyers
Tactics:
Allow buyers/middlemen to send offers directly


Enable optional quick listing (low effort input)


Provide simple notification system for new offers


Target 3: Increase Farmers’ Choice and Flexibility
Pain: Farmers rely on a single selling channel
Tactics:
Let farmers compare multiple offers in one place


Allow both selling options:


via middlemen


direct to buyers


No forced change in current selling behavior


Target 4: Lower Operational Burden
Pain: Farmers cannot handle selling + logistics
Tactics:
Keep listing process simple (minimal input)


Integrate external logistics providers


Allow middlemen to continue handling delivery


Target 5: Create a More Competitive Market
Pain: Limited competition leads to unfair pricing
Tactics:
Enable multiple buyers to access the same supply


Increase price visibility across participants


Reduce reliance on a single intermediary
